Type: Appetizer

Servings: 1

6

3

Preparation

First mix the eggs in a bowl, then put the eggs in the hot and covered with oil pan. Then spread the cheese all over the eggs in the pan and wait until the cheese melt.

Ingredients

Eggs, Bread, Oil, Cheese
